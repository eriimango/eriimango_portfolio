About Me

Hello! My name is Ericka Howard and I love to create things from code. My journey started at a young age when I helped built my first computer with my dad at 9. My dad exposed me and my siblings to technology before we could even walk. My first introduction to coding with HTML and CSS came from pimping out my myspace page, then creating custom Tumblr pages. Little did I know this was not average for a girl like me!


During my school age years I picked up a dual passion of visual arts. I always wondered how I can integrate both in to a cool future. My dream was to attend Parsons School and Design in New York for college but life and the economy did not allow me to see that dream through. I decided to attend a local college to pursue a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science concentrating in software engineering. Today I am set to start a dual Masters in Science in Data Engineering and A.I in the coming Spring of 2022(January 2022).


My goal and passion is to impact my community through technology and developmental sciences. I want to build a world, I would have loved to live in as a kid. If I could package my journey into a bottle my brand would be "Create the change I want to see."
